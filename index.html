Metasyntactic Variables

"Used to name entities such as variables, functions, and commands whose exact identity is unimportant and serve only to demonstrate a concept, which is useful for teaching programming."

source: wikipedia

      const foo = true;
      var bar = "hello";
      let baz = 1;

origin of foo and bar

foo and bar come from the military slang term

FUBAR

F****d/Fouled Up

Beyond

All Recognition/Any Repair/All Reason

origin of baz

baz most likely comes from an exclamation ("Fazz bazz!") used by Albert Alligator from the comic strip "Pogo"
